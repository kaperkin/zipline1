About Me

I am a Portland, OR native. My love for farming began when I started volunteering at Old MacDonald's farm when I was in High School. After high school, I began volunteering at the Oregon Zoo, and for the next four years I volunteered at the goat barn every Tuesday.

Growing up in a city, and living in an apartment as an adult, I didn't have much opportunity to raise farm animals myself. Besides farming, these are some other things I enjoy:

Things I enjoy:

  • Board games
  • Umbrella drinks
  • Taking care of animals
  • Science fiction

I was so excited when a former teacher told me they were moving to a tract of land, and was even more excited when she offered me the opportunity to live there and try my hand at raising farm animals. My first year, I raised 12 large, healthy, delicious turkeys! I love raising turkeys and would love to provide one to you for your Thanksgiving dinner!

Thanksgiving Turkeys

Turkeys range between 10-20 pounds depending on their appetite. I raise my turkeys as humanely as possible. They enjoy the run of the farm, are fed an organic vegetarian diet. Every evening I tuck them in and sing them a lullaby. They are never fed growth hormones, antibiotics, or other icky things. Hear the lullaby I sing here
